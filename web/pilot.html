Public-data boundary

Do not post or send:

  • private manuscripts, article PDFs, or confidential review queries;
  • patient, participant, sample, or unpublished experimental data;
  • API keys, credentials, cookies, institutional access details, or private repository links.

Identifier-only input is not proof that an identifier exists in an external registry. “Verifiable” refers to local integrity, provenance, normalized identifiers, and recorded processing decisions—not scientific or clinical validation.
